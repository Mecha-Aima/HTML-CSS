Artists & Sculptors

Javier Morales

Role: Abstract Artist

Expertise: Mixed Media, Urban Visions

Javier Morales, our abstract visionary from Barcelona,
is a maestro of unconventional patterns and bold
design. His work challenges artistic norms, inviting
viewers to explore the depths of imagination and
appreciate the beauty of the unexpected.

Athena Kyriakou

Role: Greek-style Sculpture

Expertise: Classical Revival

Athena Kyriakou, a master of Greek sculpture, infuses her
creations with the timeless beauty of ancient Greece,
based in Athens, Greece, her digital sculptures pay
homage to classical Greek art while adding a modern
touch creating a captivating fusion.

Alessandro Rossi

Role: Painter

Expertise: Digital Paintings, Ethereal Landscapes

Alessandro Rossi is a digital painter extraordinaire,
hailing from the artistic haven of Florence, Italy. Her creations seemlessly blend realism and imagination,
transporting viewers to etheral landscapes
on the digital canvas.
